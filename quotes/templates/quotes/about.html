Mike Tyson is a former American professional boxer who is widely regarded as one of the greatest heavyweight fighters of all time. Known for his aggressive style, incredible punching power, and quick knockouts, Tyson became the youngest heavyweight champion in history at age 20. He held the undisputed world heavyweight title and was famous for his intimidating presence in the ring. Despite a career marked by personal and legal challenges, Tyson remains an iconic figure in the sport of boxing.
